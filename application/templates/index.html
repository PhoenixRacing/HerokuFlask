About Us

Phoenix Racing is a student team from Olin College of Engineering. We compete in the intercollegiate Baja SAE design series, which involves designing, building, testing, and racing an off-road vehicle. Our team has 35 undergraduate stdents from all classes. We are education-oriented and provide team members with long-term experience in an extensive, hands-on project. Additionally, students get to work on design, manufacturing, testing, fixing, and even racing our vehicle!
