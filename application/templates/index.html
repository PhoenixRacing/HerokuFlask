Phoenix Racing

Phoenix Racing competes in SAE’s MiniBaja, an annual series of off-road challenges. Our team is completely student run with upper- and under-class technical leads and design managers. We’re a long term project with ongoing student involvement and high retention rates. Each year, the car is designed and fabricated entirely by students.
